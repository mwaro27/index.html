About Us: Our Journey to Cleaner Carpets

CleanSweep Solutions Service began as an online venture in Kirgiti, driven by a passion for pristine carpets and exceptional customer service. With two years of dedicated experience in the industry, we've honed our techniques and built a reputation for excellence. We are committed to continuous improvement, always seeking new ways to deliver the best results for your home or business.

While we currently operate online, our vision extends to establishing a physical location, allowing us to serve you even better. Our journey is built on trust, quality, and the promise of a truly clean sweep.
